Overview

Dr.Books is a platform dedicated to encouraging people to read by building a digital library. Same as a traditional one,we provide free access to researchers, historians, scholars, people with print disabilities, and the general public. Unlike a traditional library, we give you the chance to race, get scores and earn by reading.
